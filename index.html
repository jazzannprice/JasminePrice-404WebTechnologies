About Me

Hello, I’m Jasmine!

I am a student currently studying digital media design at Plymouth University! I aspire to go down the design route once I graduate, as I especially enjoy drawing and creating animation. However, I do also have previous experiences in various coding languages, such as Python, C and JavaScript. I plan to learn more on how to work with a graphics tablet, as taking my art down the digital route is something that interests me. My portfolio includes various examples of my art, animation and university work so far.
